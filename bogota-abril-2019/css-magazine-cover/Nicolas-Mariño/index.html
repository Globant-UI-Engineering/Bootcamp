Bizarre Sierpinski Triangle

The Sierpinski triangle

may be

constructed

from an equilateral triangle by repeated removal of triangular subsets: Each removed triangle (a trema) is topologically an open set. This process of recursively removing triangles is an example of a finite subdivision rule.
